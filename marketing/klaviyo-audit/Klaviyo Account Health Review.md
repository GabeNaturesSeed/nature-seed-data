### Top Opportunity: Winback Flow Fix

The Winback flow has 774 recipients but only 1 conversion (0.13%). With 43.7% open rate, people are reading — they're just not clicking (1.4%). The offer or CTA is failing. Emails 2+ have 0% click rate.

**Recommended actions:**

- Rewrite CTAs with specific product recommendations based on past purchase category
- Add a 3rd email with "last chance" urgency
- Split by RFM segment (At Risk vs Lapsed) with different messaging intensity

## 7. Recommendations (Priority Order)

### Critical

1. **Fix Winback Flow** — 774 sends, 1 conversion. Needs discount incentive, category-specific product recs, and stronger CTAs. This is the biggest missed opportunity.
2. **Increase List Growth** — Net -558 subscribers YTD. Add:
   - Exit-intent popup with 10% off first order
   - Checkout email opt-in (default on)
   - Yard Plan tool as lead magnet (already driving 77 subs in March)

### High Impact

3. **Improve Upsell Flow** — 0.25% conversion rate on 1,221 sends. Test:
   - Category-based product recommendations instead of generic upsell
   - Timing adjustment (current cadence may be too early/late)
   - Add social proof / reviews from same category
4. **Scale Browse Abandonment** — 68% open rate and 2.43% conversion is strong but only 392 recipients. Ensure tracking captures all browse events.
5. **Segment campaigns more aggressively** — Targeted sends deliver 7x better RPR than blasts. Use RFM × Category matrix for all future campaigns.

### Optimization

6. **Welcome Series needs volume** — Only 114 recipients = list growth problem. Once acquisition improves, test a 4-5 email series with category quiz/recommendation.
7. **A/B test subject lines** — High open rate variance (24% to 78%) suggests subject line is the primary driver. Systematize testing.
8. **Cross-sell by category** — 1.78 items/order is decent but improvable. Create flows: Lawn → Planting Aids, Pasture → Individual Species, Wildflower → Tackifier.
