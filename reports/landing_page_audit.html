How to Fix Impression Share Loss to Rank

1. Landing Page Experience (affects QS most heavily)

  • Page speed: Run PageSpeed Insights on every landing page. Target 90+ mobile score. Compress images, defer JS, use next-gen formats
  • Mobile experience: Google evaluates mobile-first. Ensure tap targets, readable text, no horizontal scroll
  • Content relevance: Landing page content must match the keyword intent. "grass seed" should land on a grass seed category page, not homepage
  • Above-the-fold CTA: Clear product/category presentation with visible add-to-cart or shop-now without scrolling
  • HTTPS + no interstitials: Ensure all pages are secure with no popup blockers on entry
  • Unique, useful content: Google rewards pages with original descriptions, reviews, guides — not just product grids

2. Ad Relevance (keyword ↔ ad copy alignment)

  • Include keyword in headlines: RSA should have at least 2 headlines containing the exact keyword or close variant
  • Tighter ad groups: If one ad group covers "grass seed" AND "wildflower seed", split them — each needs its own ad copy
  • Use keyword insertion: {Keyword:Default Text} in headlines for broad match ad groups
  • Pin relevant headlines: Pin keyword-matching headlines to positions 1-2 so they always show

3. Expected CTR (ad attractiveness in SERP)

  • Compelling headlines: Use numbers, urgency, differentiators ("Free Shipping", "Since 1998", "USDA Certified")
  • Sitelinks + callouts: Ad extensions improve CTR significantly. Ensure all campaigns have 4+ sitelinks and callout extensions
  • Structured snippets: Add "Types:", "Brands:", "Services:" snippets
  • Price extensions: Show product prices directly in the ad for shopping intent keywords
  • Review / seller ratings: If Shopper Approved reviews are connected, these boost CTR

4. PMax-Specific Fixes (no keyword QS, but feed quality matters)

  • Product feed titles: Include top search terms in product titles (e.g., "Premium Kentucky Bluegrass Seed" not just "KBG Mix")
  • High-quality images: Lifestyle images outperform white-background product shots in PMax
  • Product descriptions: Match common search queries in descriptions — Google uses these for relevance matching
  • Asset groups: Create separate asset groups for different product categories with tailored headlines/images
  • Audience signals: Add purchase intent audiences and competitor URL signals
