Theme Attribution Analysis

New theme deployed Friday, Feb 27, 2026 — comparing PRE Feb 14–27 vs POST Feb 28 – Mar 12 vs YoY 2025

Attribution Verdict

The evidence strongly suggests the new theme is a significant driver of the sales increase. 4 of 6 signals point to improved site experience (better conversion rate, lower bounce rate, organic outperforming paid). Seasonality is a contributing factor but the theme appears to be adding lift beyond what seasonal patterns alone would explain.

Evidence Signals

✅ 2026 revenue lift (+70%) exceeds 2025 seasonal lift (+10%) by 60pp — something beyond seasonality is driving growth.
✅ Conversion rate improved +1.18pp (1.03% → 2.21%). Better site experience converts more visitors — likely theme effect.
⚙️ Bounce rate increased +11.6pp — possible theme issue or more casual traffic.
✅ Organic clicks (+18%) growing faster than impressions (+6%) — improved CTR, likely better snippets/site.
⚙️ Ad spend increased +17% — part of revenue growth is from more ad investment (strategy).
✅ Direct traffic revenue up +34% — returning customers engaging more (brand + theme).
